Drug Delivery

Revolutionizing healthcare with real-time drug administration and personalized medicine.

01 // The Problem

Traditional drug administration is time-based, not biomarker-driven, resulting in:

  • Fixed, non-adaptive dosing that ignores real-time physiology
  • Peak-trough fluctuations leading to ineffective or toxic drug levels
  • Interpatient variability where identical doses have different effects on different people

02 // Dynamic Drug Delivery

A Shift from Fixed to Responsive Medicine

A real-time drug administration system should dynamically adjust dosage, timing, and frequency based on continuous biomarker monitoring. This ensures:

  • Optimal therapeutic levels without overdosing
  • Precise timing of drug release based on real-time patient needs
  • Personalized medicine at scale

Our microneedle bio-wearable, which continuously tracks real-time biomarkers, serves as the input layer for real-time drug modulation. Read more.
