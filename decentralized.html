Decentralized Data

Empowering users with secure, private, and user-controlled health data.

01 // A $1.5T Problem

30% of the world’s stored data is healthcare related, yet 50% of tests are repeated due to inaccessible records.

$800B is lost annually due to inefficiencies, fraud, and lack of interoperability.

Traditional healthcare infrastructure stores patient data in centralized servers, leading to:

  • Vulnerability to breaches & cyberattacks
  • Monopolization by hospitals & insurance firms, preventing user access
  • Data sold to third parties without consent, generating billions for corporations

This results in data silos, lack of transparency, and patient disempowerment.

02 // Solution

At Type 3 Civilization Technologies, we are pioneering a Decentralized Health Data Ecosystem that empowers users to:

  • Own and control their medical data through a blockchain-secured architecture
  • Monetize access to research organizations via smart contract-enabled transactions
  • Ensure privacy and security through zero-knowledge proof encryption

By integrating decentralized identity verification, blockchain-powered health records, and AI-driven insights, we establish a sovereign health data model where users—not corporations—own their medical information.

04 // Technology Framework

Our Decentralized Health Data Ecosystem is built on three pillars:

  • Blockchain-Powered Health Records: Immutable, encrypted patient data stored on a private ledger
  • Zero-Knowledge Proof Encryption: Ensuring selective disclosure of medical history without revealing sensitive data
  • Tokenized Data-Sharing Mechanism: Monetization of data access through smart contracts

Smart Contract-Based Data Monetization

Users can opt to share health data with research institutions & AI-driven clinical trials in exchange for tokenized incentives.
