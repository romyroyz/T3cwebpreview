Biowearable: Microneedle-Based Real-Time Biomarker Monitoring

At the core of our biowearable technology lies a revolutionary microneedle sensor array, designed to seamlessly integrate with the human body for real-time biochemical tracking. This breakthrough innovation enables continuous monitoring of biomarkers, empowering individuals and healthcare providers with unprecedented insights into health and wellness.

Key Features of Our Microneedle Biowearable

  • Painless and Non-Invasive: Our microneedles are engineered to be virtually painless, ensuring a comfortable user experience.
  • Real-Time Data Collection: The sensor array continuously monitors biomarkers such as glucose, lactate, cortisol, and cardiac markers, providing real-time feedback.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Data is transmitted via BLE and LORA protocols, enabling seamless integration with mobile apps and cloud platforms.
  • Long-Lasting and Reliable: Designed for extended wear, the biowearable ensures consistent performance over time.

How It Works

The microneedle sensor array is embedded in a wearable patch that sits on the skin. Each microneedle is coated with an enzymatic layer that reacts with specific biomarkers in the interstitial fluid. The resulting electrochemical signals are processed by an onboard microcontroller and transmitted wirelessly to a connected device.

Integration with Dynamic Drug Delivery

Our biowearable is seamlessly integrated with the Dynamic Drug Delivery System, enabling real-time adjustments to medication based on biomarker data. This ensures precise, personalized treatment with no risk of overdosing or inefficiencies.

Decentralized Health Data Ecosystem

All data collected by the biowearable is securely stored in our Decentralized Health Data Ecosystem. This blockchain-based system ensures that users retain full control over their data, with the option to share it with researchers or healthcare providers for monetization or personalized care.
