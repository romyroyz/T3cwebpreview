03 // HOW IT WORKS

3.1 | The Biowearable

Our biowearable device uses a microneedle sensor array to monitor your biochemistry in real-time. Painless, non-invasive, and always on—it’s like having a lab on your skin.

3.2 | Adaptive Drug System

Say goodbye to fixed doses. Our adaptive drug system releases medication only when your body needs it. Using nanofluidic technology and AI-driven algorithms, we ensure precise, efficient, and safe treatment.

3.3 | Encrypted Blockchain Data Layer

Your health data is yours alone. Stored on a decentralized blockchain, it’s encrypted, secure, and fully under your control. Share it with researchers, monetize it, or keep it private—it’s your choice.

04 // THE TECHNOLOGY BEHIND IT

4.1 | Microneedle Biosensing Architecture

Our microneedle biosensors are a marvel of engineering. They use:

  • An electrochemical transduction layer for accurate readings.
  • Enzymatic coatings for molecular recognition.
  • BLE and LORA protocols for seamless data transmission.

4.2 | Precision Drug Delivery System

Our drug delivery system is smarter than ever. It features:

  • Nano-mesh actuators for precise control.
  • Polymeric membranes that adapt to your body’s needs.
  • AI algorithms that optimize drug release in real-time.

4.3 | Decentralized Data Ownership

Your data is secure and private, thanks to:

  • Private blockchain and zero-knowledge encryption.
  • A self-sovereign architecture that puts you in control.
  • Tokenized sharing for fair compensation.
